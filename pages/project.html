This project was completed by me alongside three other people during our Fundamentals of Engineering Design Innovation class. Our initial goal was to create some sort of system that would help improve the social and academic experience of the students at CU Denver.

The Process

After taking some time to consider our own ideas and perform interviews with potential users, we determined that a website would be a useful medium to attempt to solve our problem. We created a simple prototype using pencil and paper and did some testing.

A compilation of our initial ideation, prototyping, and user testing

After a process of refining our ideas and concepts, we made two realizations:

  1. We should focus on specifically academic communication and resource access.
  2. There is no reason to limit this only to students at CU Denver.
With that, we settled on designing a website that provided an open forum for asking and answering academic questions, and made it easy for students to share and find academic resources. We produced several higher-fidelity prototypes and did some more user testing.
